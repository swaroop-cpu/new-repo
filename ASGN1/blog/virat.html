Virat Kohli - A Cricketing Legend

About Virat Kohli

Virat Kohli is an Indian cricketer who is widely regarded as one of the greatest batsmen of all time.
He is the current captain of the Indian national team in all formats and is known for his aggressive batting style,
impeccable technique, and unwavering passion for the game.

  • Born: November 5, 1988, Delhi, India
  • Playing role: Right-handed batsman
  • Batting style: Right-handed
  • Teams: India, Royal Challengers Bangalore

Career Highlights

  • Over 12,000 runs in ODIs, with an average over 58
  • Over 8,000 runs in Test cricket, with an average over 50
  • Most centuries by an Indian in ODIs and second most overall
  • Captained India to victory in the 2011 Cricket World Cup
  • Led India to the top of the Test rankings in 2016

Awards and Recognition

  • ICC Cricketer of the Year (2017, 2018)
  • ICC ODI Player of the Year (2012, 2017, 2018)
  • ICC Test Player of the Year (2018)
  • Wisden Leading Cricketer in the World (2016, 2017, 2018)
  • Padma Shri (2017)
