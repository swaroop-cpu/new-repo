The Spicy Noodles


Ingredients :

  1. pack instant ramen noodles (seasoning packet not needed)
  2. shallots/green onions
  3. garlic cloves
  4. tbsp oil

Sauce

  1. 1 tsp sweet chilli sauce
  2. 1 tsp light soy sauce
  3. tsp dark soy sauce
  4. tsp water




Instructions :

Step 1
Thinly slice 2 shallots/green onions. Shallots are preferred as they are sweeter but green onions work too
Step 2
Mince 2 cloves of garlic. Add more if you like a stronger garlic flavour
Step 3
Prepare the sauce and set aside
Step 4
On low flame, fry shallots/green onion in 3 tbsp oil until golden and crisp. Take it off the pan when it turns pale gold or it will burn and taste bitter
Step 5
Cook 1 pack of instant ramen noodles as per package instructions. Drain and set aside
Step 6
Retain 1 tbsp oil from the pan used to fry the shallots/green onions. The remaining oil is flavourful and can be used in other dishes
Step 7
On low flame, saut√© minced garlic for 30 seconds or until lightly golden
Step 8
Pour in the prepared sauce and simmer for 30 seconds
Step 9
Add the cooked ramen noodles and mix quickly
Step 10
Stir fry for just 30 seconds or the noodles will get mushy
Step 11
Add ramen noodles to a serving bowl, garnish with crispy fried onion and green onions. Enjoy!
